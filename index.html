Rules of the game

Players take turns and clock on the buttons. It is imperative to finish the row that you started. You can press a maximum of 3 bubbles at the same time (they must be near, you cannot press several pimples at once, if they are not close to each other). The task is to make the opponent go last. Who went last - he lost!
